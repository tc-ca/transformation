TC Transformation: Here’s what you missed in September!

With so many of us working from outside the office, the skills, tools and processes that enable remote work have never been as important as they are today. Even those of us who are working from our regular worksite have also had to adjust to working alongside remote teams and serving remote clients.

While TC as a whole has had a very successful transition to this new reality, many of us have also experienced new challenges – both in our work and personal lives – as we’ve adjusted to this change.

Throughout September, we brought conversations about remote work at TC out in the open. Here is a recap of what happened this month:


Remote Work Webinar Series

Subject matter experts from across the department put their knowledge on display with a series of webinars that looked at the many angles of remote work at TC. These sessions gave us important guidance on how to keep close with our teams, and provided insights into more specific areas impacted by remote work, such as training and inspections. There was even information shared on some very helpful tips and tricks for working remotely!

Don’t worry if you missed any of the webinars, they were recorded and are available at the following links:

  • Establishing Social Connections with Remote Teams (bilingual session, French-only session)
  • Working Remotely – Tools, Tips and Tricks
  • Remote Inspections
  • Onboarding and Offboarding in a Remote Work Environment
  • Virtual Training

Executive Panel on Remote Work

TC’s Chief Digital Officer, Julie Leese, moderated an engaging cross-country discussion with three of her colleagues on TC’s senior executive team. They talked about the advantages, challenges, and future of remote work at TC. They also compared notes on their own personal experiences over the past six months. If you missed this thought-provoking discussion, it’s not too late to watch it now!
